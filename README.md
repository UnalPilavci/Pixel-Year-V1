## ğŸŒŸ About the Project

**Pixel Year** is a minimalist personal development assistant that transforms your emotional awareness into a work of art. Bringing the "Year in Pixels" concept to the digital world, the application helps you visualize a broad emotional map of your life by representing each day with a unique color.

> *"Don't just feel your emotions; turn them into a visual story by adding a pixel every day."*
